About Us

Designing Spaces With Purpose & Simplicity

At Plenara Studio, we believe good design is not just about aesthetics — it's about creating spaces that enhance everyday living. We specialize in architecture, interiors, turnkey execution, and modular solutions for premium homes and mid-luxury residences across India. Our design language is rooted in minimalism, timelessness, and functionality. Every project is carefully planned to reflect the personality and lifestyle of its homeowners while maintaining a refined and modern aesthetic. With a strong focus on detail, materiality, and execution quality, we ensure every space feels cohesive, practical, and visually balanced.

The Visionaries

Meet The Founders

Disha & Vipul

Founders of Plenara Studio

Founded by passionate designers with a vision to create refined and functional homes, Plenara Studio blends creativity with practical execution.

With over 9 years of experience in the modular furniture and kitchen industry, the studio has built a reputation for thoughtful planning, quality craftsmanship, and modern timeless interiors.

The founders have been recognized with the PAN India North Zone Best Kitchen Designer Award twice in the last three years.

Their design philosophy focuses on creating homes that feel calm, elegant, and deeply connected to the people living in them.
